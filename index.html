        How to send    +
        
    
          
  1. To your Chief Minister. Find the CMO contact at your state government's website — search "[Your State] CM grievance" or "[Your State] CMO email". Most state CMOs publish a public email or a contact form. (Coming soon: a verified per-state directory in this letter.)
    2. 
          
  2. To the Prime Minister. Use the official PM contact form at pmindia.gov.in/en/interact-with-honble-pm ↗. The PMO does not publish a direct email, but every submission via the form is logged.
    3. 
          
  3. For an audit trail. File the same complaint via the central grievance portal at pgportal.gov.in ↗ (CPGRAMS) — every submission gets a docket number and a response timeline. Address it to the Ministry of Culture and your State CMO.
    4. 
          
  4. Click Open in mail to draft the email if you have your CM's address. Otherwise Copy text and paste into the portal forms.
    5. 
          
  5. Send. Then send to a friend.

            Hero figure (₹4.66)
            Real per-Indian library spend for 2024-25, expressed in constant 2011-12 ₹ via the MoSPI implicit GDP deflator (National Accounts Statistics, base 2011-12) — the standard for real government-spending analysis per IMF GFSM 2014, OECD Government at a Glance, World Bank PER methodology, and RBI State Finances. Computed by extending Kulkarni-Balaji-Dhanamjaya (2025) state-level series with corrected TG 2020 annual population projections (MoHFW) and CAGR (2016-17→2020-21) extrapolation where CAG actuals are not yet published. Real-terms peak: ₹7.87 in 2018-19. Methodology and per-state CAGRs at /spend/.

        Notes on the data
        Census 2011 population basis used for per-capita calculations in source data. West Bengal 2019-20 (₹0.46) reflects reporting irregularity. Tripura 2020-21 (₹0.07) requires further investigation. Many of the quantitative ratios are advocacy-grade approximations rounded to the nearest order of magnitude; methodological exactness varies. Where multiple figures exist, the most recent CAG-verified or peak-year value has been used.
